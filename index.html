about us

We Are The Leader in Kitchen Design, Tombstones and Construction

Stonecites is a wholly Zambian owned company that was established with an idea of product and services driven by customer needs. The business was incorporated in the year 2003 and has grown from an enterprise to a limited company with an increased product type and volume of produces and services sold. The company manufactures kitchen cabinets, built-in cabinets and various cabinetry. The company is the major supplier of granite, cafe stone, ceaser stone, kitchen accessories and hardware, the company is also specialized in the field of civil works, general construction and supplies. The business was primarily established to provide quality, reliable, durable and effective products to the government and private sectors of Zambia.
